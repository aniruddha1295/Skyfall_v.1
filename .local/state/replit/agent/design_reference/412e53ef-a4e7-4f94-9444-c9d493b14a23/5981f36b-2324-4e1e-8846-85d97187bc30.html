Frequently Asked Questions

Weather derivatives are financial instruments whose value is derived from weather-related variables like rainfall, temperature, or wind speed. They allow you to hedge against or speculate on weather events. In our platform, options pay out based on actual weather measurements from WeatherXM's decentralized network.
Weather data comes from WeatherXM's network of 7,000+ verified weather stations globally. Data is delivered on-chain via Chainlink oracles with cryptographic proof. All settlements are automatic and transparent, with full audit trails available in the Payout History section.
Individual options are traditional derivatives where you pay a premium for potential payout. Community pools are mutual aid funds where members contribute to a shared pool that pays out to affected members during weather events. Pools often have governance features and can use different distribution methods.
Our AI uses Monte Carlo simulation with 5+ years of historical weather data to calculate fair option prices. The system runs 10,000+ scenarios and achieves 94.2% pricing accuracy. All AI decisions include transparent rationale explanations, and you can always see the methodology in the Analytics section.
